About Black Plague Studios

One roof for three crafts.

Black Plague Studios (Pty) Ltd is a creative studio specializing in animation, game design, and writing. We founded the company after identifying a clear gap in the market where these three creative disciplines are often siloed.

Our goal is to combine them under one roof to offer cohesive and comprehensive digital content services — where each discipline sharpens the others instead of working alone.

Our Story

Born from late-night conversations.

Black Plague Studios was born out of late-night conversations, shared frustrations, and a mutual desire to create something bigger than ourselves. As young creatives navigating the worlds of animation, game design, and storytelling, we noticed how often these fields were disconnected — when in truth, they thrive when combined.

We built this studio not just to create content, but to build a culture — one that honours collaboration, pays people fairly, and gives young African creatives the space to shine.

We believe students should gain experience while being compensated for their efforts. Our studio model is a deliberate alternative to exploitative unpaid internships.
