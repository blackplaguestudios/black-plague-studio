# Black Plague Studios

**No Gimmicks. Just Games, Animation, and Words That Work.**

Official website for Black Plague Studios (Pty) Ltd — a South African creative studio 
combining animation, game design, and writing under one roof.

## 🎮 About

Black Plague Studios was founded to bridge the gap between studying and working in the 
creative industry — offering paid, project-based experience to young African creatives 
across animation, game design, and storytelling, all in one collaborative studio.
