Get Paid to Create

We believe students should gain experience while being compensated for their efforts. No exploitative unpaid internships — just real, credited, paid creative work.

The opportunity

Real industry experience, on fair terms.

Our studio model gives students and early-career creatives a place to do meaningful work across animation, game design, and writing — and to earn from what they build.

We started Black Plague Studios partly because we lived the gap ourselves: talented young creatives finishing their studies with nowhere to apply their skills, often expected to work for “exposure.”

So we built the alternative — a studio where contributions are paid, credited, and owned by the people who make them.
